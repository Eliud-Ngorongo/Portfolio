Project information

  • Category: Software Integration.
  • Client: Marina Stores.
  • Project date: 01, January 2019.

Problem and Solution

Marina Stores is an animal feeds supplier in rural Kenya. They source, stock and supply feeds to farmers within their locality at no transportaion cost and having been doing so for the last 18 years.

Manual record keeping was a big pain point for the founders as it hindered proper tracking of invoices and business expenses.

I was the project lead for sourcing, installing and maintaining the Sage50 ERP system which has reduced redudancies at the company as well as grown the customer base by 39% yearly. I was also in charge of training the sales and accounts team on how to use the new system.

This taught me a great deal of patience and the need to be highly flexible when it comes to training different mindsets.
